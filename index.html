Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our QuickBooks conversion services

What QuickBooks file formats can you convert?

We can convert between all major QuickBooks file formats including QBW (Company File), QBA (Accountant's Copy), QBB (Backup File), and QBO (Online File). We also specialize in converting QuickBooks Accountant files to Enterprise versions.

How long does the conversion process take?

Most standard conversions are completed within 24-48 hours. Complex conversions or those involving very large files may take slightly longer. We'll provide a time estimate once we've reviewed your file.

Is my financial data secure?

Absolutely. We take data security very seriously. All files are transferred through secure encrypted channels, and we delete all client files from our systems after conversion is complete and delivered.

What if my file is corrupted or doesn't convert properly?

We have extensive experience working with damaged or corrupted QuickBooks files. If we cannot convert your file successfully, you don't pay. We'll diagnose the issue and let you know the options before proceeding.

How do I send you my files and receive the converted version?

After you contact us, we'll provide a secure upload link for your files. Once converted, we'll send you a download link for the converted files. We can also use other secure transfer methods if preferred.
